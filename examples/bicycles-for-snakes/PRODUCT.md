# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Snakes. Also: anyone who reads "zero-pedal drivetrain" and appreciates that someone did the engineering work. The landing page should look like a real premium cycling/outdoor gear brand — technical specs, craftsmanship language, outdoor photography — but the product is a bicycle for a species with no limbs.

## Product Purpose

Bicycles for Snakes makes mobility hardware for the limbless. Two wheels, zero legs, infinite ambition. The product is technically rigorous — scale-friendly handlebars, extra-long frame geometry, forkless confidence mode. The brand takes the design challenge completely seriously. Success: someone reads the spec sheet and wonders if it would actually work.

## Brand Personality

Technical, earnest, slightly missionary. Like a premium outdoor gear company that found an underserved market and went all in. Three words: precise, committed, earnest.

## Anti-references

Not cartoonish snake illustration. Not toy branding. Not generic e-commerce. Not REI generic outdoorsy.

## Design Principles

1. Engineering language applied faithfully — spec sheets, material callouts, technical diagrams.
2. The product is real until proven otherwise — treat it as a genuine hardware company.
3. Nature-meets-machine aesthetic — outdoor gear meets precision engineering.
4. The absurdity is structural, not visual — the design never winks, the product brief does.
5. Details reward attention — the more specific the spec, the funnier and more credible.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum.
