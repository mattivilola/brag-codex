# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Fish with ambition. Also: anyone who sees "the sky was never the limit, it was just poorly onboarded" and feels seen. The landing page should look like a real flight school or aviation academy — institutional, aspirational, precise — except the students are fish.

## Product Purpose

Fish Flight School offers flight lessons, cloud navigation, altitude tracking, and wing certification to fish who are ready to leave the water behind. The school takes its mission seriously — beginner flapping lessons, emergency splash landing protocols, full curriculum. Success: someone reads "I used to fear the sky. Now I fear birds, weather, and gravity." and immediately enrolls.

## Brand Personality

Institutional, aspirational, quietly cinematic. Like a real aviation academy that believes deeply in its mission and happens to accept aquatic applicants. Three words: ambitious, rigorous, inspiring.

## Anti-references

Not Finding Nemo aesthetic. Not children's book illustration. Not generic motivational poster. Not flight simulation software UI.

## Design Principles

1. Aviation academy conventions followed faithfully — curriculum structure, certification language, altitude data.
2. Aspiration over irony — the school genuinely believes fish can fly, and the design reflects that belief.
3. Sky as the world — blues, clouds, altitude, the infinite vertical frontier.
4. Cinematic scale — this is a grand mission, the layout should feel like a destination.
5. The testimonials carry the bit — specific, character-voiced, absurd in exactly the right way.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum.
